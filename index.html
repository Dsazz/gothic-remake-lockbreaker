Gothic Lock Breaker

A clean-path solver for the Gothic 1 Remake locks. Drives every pin to the notch without ever grinding one against the frame.

I · The Lock

II · Starting Pins

Mark where each pin rests before you begin. Dead-center (0) is the goal; -3 and +3 are the two walls. Enter each pin on the side it actually sits in-game, and later turn each plate the way the arrow points on screen — keep both in the same frame and the path stays clean.

III · Coupled Tumblers

Pick the column of the tumbler you turn, then mark how each other tumbler (its row) reacts. Leave a cell blank if it stays put; With means it turns the same way; Against means it turns the opposite way.
